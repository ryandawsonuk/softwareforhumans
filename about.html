About Me

I like to ask the why questions behind the how questions. Why approaches work and why they have limitations.

I believe this pays off in the long run. We jump too quickly to answers.

Prior to that I was consulting on Java projects across big banks. That was Java projects of lots of shapes and sizes.

My PhD and academic work is on Philsophy of Mathematics. It can be found on academia.edu
